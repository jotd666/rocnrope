"# Roc'NRope" 
port of Roc'NRope for Amiga

Requires a 2-button joystick, or the keyboard

Features:

- 50 fps on unexpanded A1200 (using dual playfield 16+16 colors + hardware sprites)
- 25 fps on unaccelerated A500 1MB (using 16 colors + hardware sprites)

Instructions:

fire/5: insert coin
up/down/1/2: start game
joystick/arrows: move
red/ctrl: shoot grappling hook
blue/alt: dazzle
ESC: quit current game

Cheat keys:

F1: skip level
F3: add 10000 points
F4: toggle invincibility

How to play:

You are an explorer in search of the elusive Phoenix in order to return its feathers. 
You move from one platform to another by shooting a harpoon cable that connects 
to both platforms. You must be careful not to run into any of the creatures or cavemen patrolling the platforms. 
Use your flashlight to briefly stun them or to shake them off the cables if they start climbing after you on them.

Get to the top. Get golden feathers for bonus.

You die if you encounter a caveman, dinosaur or pterodactyl, fall from too high,
or get crushed by a boulder that the pterodactyl drops. Eggs give you a few seconds of superpower: kill the creatures
on contact (except pterodactyl that will still kill you), and flashlight also will kill them.
The enemies can also use the cables you set, and can make you fall off it by shaking it.

Note that using the flashlight on an enemy which is on the cable effectively kills him, as well as shooting another cable
when an enemy is on the old one.
